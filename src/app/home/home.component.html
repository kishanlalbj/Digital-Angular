Building Digital Engineers for the future

Wipro’s Digital arm launched the ‘Digital Academy’ in September 2015, to build a strong team of digital engineers for the future. The Academy aims to nurture excellent technologists and programmers who have the ability to completely build and release substantial internet scale applications.

Digital Academy, is powered by a vision to create “Excellent Programmers” who have the ability to completely build and release substantial applications. It provides a platform to upskill and, transform an individual, by building multi-dimensional perspective that includes Entrepreneurship, Design thinking, Performance, and support differential thinking.

Digital Academy Offerings

Digital Academy runs one of the world’s best software engineering boot camps that produces confident full-stack developers and machine learning programmers . At Digital Academy, each program is designed to ensure outcomes that are relevant to business are achieved.

Proof of competency in the Stack Route immersive full time program is demonstrated by building and releasing products and technology – a journey that is tough.

This real and tough experience transforms engineers into confident professionals who can lead customer engagements.Over the last 18 months, Digital Academy has successfully graduated over 470 full-stack developers.

What sets us apart

Real Experiences

Building and releasing the real product with tough deadlines and engineering practices transforms from hesitant developers to confident developers who are willing to take up the challenges.

Flipped Classroom

Participants do self-study using a set of videos/materials. These are supplemented by in-classroom discussion. The StackRoute development environment mandates the use of TDD and Continuous Integration.

Immersive, Apprenticeship and completeness

All learn to be full-stackers under Apprenticeship with practitioner. Code is reviewed to provide a powerful learning experience that stays with participants for a long time.

Open ended and exploratory rather than prescriptive

From building app-fabrics or micro-services or real-time stream handing or graph based system, we take techology to a different level.
